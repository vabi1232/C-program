The briards ,or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog tradditionally
used as a herder and guardian of sheep.

Breed History

The briad, which is belived to have originate in France,hass been bred
for centuries to herd and to proted sheep.The breed was used by the
French Army as sentries, messengers and to search for wounded
solidies because of its fine sense of hearing. Briards were used in the
First World War almost to the point of extincion.Currently the population
of briards is slowly recovering. charlemage,Napoleon,Thomas
jefferson and lafayette all owned briards.
